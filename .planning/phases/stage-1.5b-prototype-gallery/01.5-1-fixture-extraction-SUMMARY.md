## Deviations from plan (Auto-fixed Rule 1 issues)

Three sub-bugs in the substitution gate were discovered during fallback-mode dry-run and auto-fixed inline. All committed at 299af51 with detailed rationale in the commit body.

1. **[Rule 1 — Bug] Parenthetical-annotation parser missed "outer" form.** SUBSTITUTION-MAP rows of shape `Short (Long Form Name) | Sanitized` registered only the literal `Short (Long Form Name)` key. The original parser added `Long Form Name` as inner-only; missing `Short` alone meant that a script invocation passing `Short` (e.g., `substitute("Banko Overhead Doors", subMap)`) would not match. Fixed: now registers all three forms (full + outer + inner) → same sanitized value. Caught by gate self-test on `Banko Overhead Doors` and `MJ Florida` survival.

2. **[Rule 1 — Bug] Substitution + grepGate did not use word-boundary matching.** Short keys like `501` → `712` would match inside cents amounts like `5012` or `1501`, corrupting numeric data. Fixed: both `substitute()` and `grepGate()` wrap keys in `\b...\b` regex anchors. Net effect: `501 74th Street` substitutes correctly (word boundaries on both sides); `5012` is left alone.

3. **[Rule 1 — Bug] Substring-collision check false-positived on construction terms.** Denylist token `Pou` (Tier 2 customer surname) substring-matched inside `Foundation Pour`. Fixed: denylist tokens ≤4 chars use word-boundary matching; longer tokens still use substring matching to catch OCR drift like `Drummondville`.

In addition, the schedule task name `Foundation Pour` was renamed to `Foundation Casting` to avoid even the appearance of a denylist-token collision (defensive, not strictly required after fix #3 landed).
