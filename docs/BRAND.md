## Origin Story
We tried Adaptive. They wanted $2,000 a month. So we built Nightwork — the same AI-powered invoice-to-draw pipeline, built by a builder running 14 active jobs, at a quarter of the price.

## Core Values
1. Your business never sleeps — Nightwork runs 24/7 so builders don't have to. The night shift never clocks out.
2. Built by builders, not software teams — Every feature exists because a builder needed it in the field.
3. Earn it every month — No contracts, no lock-in, no hostage data. We earn your business monthly.
4. Don't miss a beat — Every invoice coded, every draw balanced, every budget current. Nothing falls through the cracks.

## Brand Concept
Nightwork represents the invisible operational work of running a building business — invoices, draws, budgets, scheduling, coordination, taxes, comms. The "nightwork" that builders do at kitchen tables after hours. The software is the ghost company running behind the scenes 24/7. Like having an extra crew that never clocks out, compressing what used to take all night into minutes.

## Voice Rules
- Lead with time saved, not technology
- Never say "revolutionize," "disrupt," or "end-to-end solution"
- Never lead with "AI-powered" — the AI is the engine, not the value prop
- Confident, not arrogant. Direct, not salesy. Builder-first, not tech-first.
- Understated, like the name — work that happens quietly and reliably
